# 🐤 Lovely Farm
**Lovely Farm** is a prototype game developed in Unity 6 for PC, focused on developing the visual component, game tempo and basic arcade mechanics. 

This is an arcade game in which the player controls a farm girl with diagonal movement and the mechanics of shooting food to feed the approaching animals with different characteristics.

## General description
The movement of the character is realized diagonally, through the A and D buttons. 
The left mouse is used to shoot food.

There are three types of animals that player should feed: 
1. Hens
2. Roosters 
3. Chicks

They all have different movement speeds and the amount of "health" that reflects the required number of feedings.
Chicks are the fastest, hens move at an average speed, and roosters are the slowest, but require more action to feed successfully.

## Game Over
For each successful feeding, the player receives points, and the process is accompanied by a confetti effect. 

If the animal passes by the player, the player loses one of the three health hearts. 
After losing all the lives, the Game Over screen is displayed with the total number of points scored.
